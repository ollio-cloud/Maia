# Create Technical Blog Post Command

## Purpose
Generate comprehensive technical thought leadership blog posts with SEO optimization, demonstrating expertise in AI implementation, business technology transformation, and enterprise architecture.

## Usage
- "Create a technical blog post about [topic]"
- "Write a thought leadership article on [subject]"
- "Generate blog content for [target audience]"

## Agent Integration
**Primary Agent**: Blog Writer Agent
**Supporting Agents**: 
- Company Research Agent (industry intelligence)
- LinkedIn AI Advisor Agent (amplification strategy)
- Prompt Engineer Agent (content optimization)

## Command Parameters
- **Topic**: Technical subject with business implications
- **Target Audience**: [enterprise_architects|business_leaders|technical_professionals|strategic_planners]
- **Content Depth**: [strategic_overview|implementation_guide|deep_technical|market_analysis]
- **SEO Focus**: Primary and secondary keyword targeting
- **Word Count**: 1,500-2,500 words for thought leadership positioning

## Content Framework

### Article Structure Template
```markdown
# [SEO-Optimized Title with Primary Keyword]

## Executive Summary
- Key insights and value proposition (150-200 words)
- Target audience pain points addressed
- Main takeaways and actionable outcomes

## The Business Context
- Industry trends and market drivers
- Current challenges facing target audience
- Why this topic matters now

## Technical Deep Dive
- Core concepts and implementation details
- Architecture patterns and best practices
- Real-world examples and case studies

## Practical Implementation
- Step-by-step guidance and frameworks
- Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
- Resource requirements and success factors

## Business Impact Analysis
- ROI considerations and value measurement
- Strategic implications for organizations
- Competitive advantages gained

## Future Implications
- Technology evolution trends
- Market disruption potential
- Preparation strategies for leaders

## Conclusion & Call to Action
- Key takeaways summary
- Next steps for readers
- Professional engagement invitation
```
