# Develop Case Study Command

## Purpose
Transform projects, achievements, and implementations into compelling business narratives with quantified results, demonstrating both technical capability and business impact through structured case study development.

## Usage
- "Develop a case study from [project/achievement]"
- "Create implementation case study for [business outcome]"
- "Document [system/process] as a case study"

## Agent Integration
**Primary Agent**: Blog Writer Agent
**Supporting Agents**:
- Personal Knowledge Graph (experience database access)
- Company Research Agent (competitive context and market intelligence)
- Financial Advisor Agent (ROI analysis and business impact quantification)

## Case Study Types & Templates

### Technology Implementation Case Study
**Focus**: System development, architecture decisions, technical innovation
**Audience**: Technical leaders, enterprise architects, engineering teams
**Emphasis**: Technical sophistication, performance outcomes, scalability achievements

**Template Example**: "Building Maia: From Concept to Production AI System"
- Challenge: Personal productivity limitations with existing AI tools
- Solution: Multi-agent orchestration system with real-time communication
- Results: 3x workflow performance, 50-80% cost reduction, 95% context retention

### Business Process Optimization Case Study
**Focus**: Workflow improvement, efficiency gains, operational transformation
**Audience**: Business leaders, operations managers, process improvement specialists
**Emphasis**: Business impact, ROI achievement, organizational change management

**Template Example**: "Portfolio Governance Transformation: 55% to 85% Team Utilization"
- Challenge: Underutilized team capacity and inefficient resource allocation
- Solution: Systematic demand intake and capacity planning framework
- Results: 30% utilization improvement, £300k cost optimization, improved delivery predictability

### Strategic Initiative Case Study
**Focus**: Organizational change, strategic transformation, capability development
**Audience**: Executive leadership, strategic planners, change management professionals
**Emphasis**: Strategic value, competitive advantage, long-term capability building

**Template Example**: "Digital Transformation Strategy: From Traditional BRM to AI-Augmented Leadership"
- Challenge: Traditional business relationship management in AI-driven market
- Solution: Personal AI infrastructure development and AI expertise positioning
- Results: Enhanced professional differentiation, expanded service capability, new market opportunities
