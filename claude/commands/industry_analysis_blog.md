## Industry Analysis Templates

### Technology Disruption Analysis
**Title Format**: "The [Technology] Revolution: Why [Industry] Leaders Must Act Now"
**Focus**: Technology impact, adoption timeline, competitive implications
**Audience**: Technology executives, strategic planners, innovation leaders

**Example**: "The Enterprise AI Revolution: Why Business Leaders Must Move Beyond Pilots"
- Market context: Current state of enterprise AI adoption
- Competitive landscape: Leaders, laggards, and emerging disruptors
- Technology impact: Capability advances and business transformation potential
- Strategic recommendations: Action plan for competitive positioning

### Market Evolution Analysis
**Title Format**: "[Industry] at an Inflection Point: Strategic Implications of [Trend]"
**Focus**: Market dynamics, competitive shifts, strategic positioning
**Audience**: Business executives, strategic planners, investors

**Example**: "Business Technology Services at an Inflection Point: The AI-Augmented Consultant"
- Market context: Traditional consulting model under pressure
- Competitive landscape: Technology-enabled service providers vs. traditional firms
- Strategic implications: Differentiation through AI capability and technology expertise
- Future vision: Evolution toward AI-augmented professional services

### Regulatory & Compliance Impact Analysis
**Title Format**: "[Regulation/Standard] Impact: Strategic Response Framework for [Industry]"
**Focus**: Compliance requirements, implementation challenges, competitive implications
**Audience**: Compliance leaders, risk managers, strategic executives

**Example**: "AI Governance Requirements: Enterprise Response Framework for Regulatory Compliance"
- Regulatory landscape: Current and emerging AI governance requirements
- Implementation challenges: Technical, organizational, and resource considerations
- Strategic response: Framework for proactive compliance and competitive advantage
- Future implications: Compliance as strategic differentiation opportunity
