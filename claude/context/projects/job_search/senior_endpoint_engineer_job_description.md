# Senior Endpoint Engineer

## Position Overview

We are seeking an experienced Senior Endpoint Engineer to lead the design, implementation, and optimization of our enterprise endpoint management strategy. This role is critical in ensuring the security, compliance, and productivity of our diverse device ecosystem while driving innovation in modern workplace technologies. The ideal candidate will combine deep technical expertise with strategic thinking to deliver scalable endpoint solutions that support our zero-trust security model and enhance user experience across the organization.

## Key Responsibilities

### Endpoint Architecture & Strategy (30%)
- Design and maintain enterprise endpoint management architecture supporting 5,000+ devices across Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android platforms
- Lead the evolution from traditional device management to modern cloud-native endpoint strategies
- Develop and implement zero-trust endpoint security frameworks with conditional access and compliance policies
- Create device lifecycle strategies encompassing provisioning, management, refresh, and retirement
- Architect hybrid solutions integrating cloud (Intune) and on-premises (SCCM) management platforms

### Security & Compliance (25%)
- Implement and manage endpoint detection and response (EDR/XDR) solutions (Microsoft Defender for Endpoint, CrowdStrike, or similar)
- Design and enforce device compliance policies including BitLocker, FileVault, and mobile device encryption
- Lead vulnerability management initiatives including patch deployment strategies and remediation workflows
- Develop endpoint-based conditional access policies integrated with identity management systems
- Ensure compliance with industry standards (ISO 27001, NIST, CIS) and regulatory requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)

### Modern Workplace Engineering (20%)
- Design and implement zero-touch provisioning using Windows Autopilot and Apple Device Enrollment Program (DEP)
- Manage application deployment strategies for Win32, MSIX, and mobile applications
- Optimize Windows Update for Business and iOS/macOS update deployment rings
- Implement BYOD and mobile application management (MAM) strategies
- Enhance user experience through self-service portals and Company Portal optimization

### Automation & Operations (15%)
- Develop PowerShell scripts and automation for endpoint management tasks
- Leverage Microsoft Graph API for programmatic device management and reporting
- Create proactive remediation scripts and automated compliance workflows
- Implement endpoint analytics and user experience monitoring
- Design automated incident response procedures for compromised devices

### Technical Leadership (10%)
- Provide tier 3 support and escalation for complex endpoint issues
- Mentor junior team members and drive knowledge sharing initiatives
- Lead endpoint-related projects from conception to implementation
- Collaborate with security, infrastructure, and application teams
- Document technical designs, procedures, and best practices

## Required Qualifications

### Education & Experience
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (or equivalent experience)
- 5+ years of experience in endpoint management and enterprise IT
- 3+ years of hands-on experience with Microsoft Intune and/or SCCM
